Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Immersed. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our focus and productivity application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and profile information when you sign up via Google authentication.
  • Usage Data: Focus session duration, session counts, and productivity metrics to help track your progress.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers for service optimization.
  • Room Activity: When using focus rooms, we store room participation data to facilitate collaboration features.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our focus tracking and productivity services
  • Display leaderboards and global statistics (using anonymized data)
  • Enable real-time collaboration in focus rooms
  • Improve our application and user experience
  • Send occasional updates about new features (only with your consent)

4. Data Storage and Security

We use Firebase for secure data storage. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Firebase Authentication with OAuth 2.0
  • Firestore security rules protecting user data
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with:

  • Service providers (Firebase/Google) necessary to operate our application
  • Other room participants when you join a public focus room (display name and focus status only)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and export your data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Update your profile information
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

7. Cookies and Local Storage

We use essential cookies and local storage to maintain your session and preferences. This includes theme settings and authentication tokens. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@getimmersed.in
